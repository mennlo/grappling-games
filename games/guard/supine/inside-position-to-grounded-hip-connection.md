# Inside Position to Grounded Hip Connection

## Overview
An asymmetric leg attack drill where the bottom player works from two different starting positions to accomplish two simultaneous goals: getting the top player's hips on the mat and establishing a connection to those hips. The top player works to pass the bottom player's leg guard by connecting their shin to the belt on the outside, staying fully standing or in base.

## Details
**Time:** 3 minutes, 2 partners or 6 minutes total - switching back and forth

### Bottom Player
**Position:** Start from either (a) On your back with feet on the inside of top player's knees/thighs and hands on both ankles, OR (b) Overhook x-guard position  
**Objective:** Get top player's hips on the mat AND make a physical connection to their hips (order doesn't matter)  
**Win:** Successfully accomplish both goals - hips on mat and hip connection established

### Top Player
**Position:** Standing with good posture (or in stable base) with bottom player attacking from below  
**Objective:** Pass the bottom player's guard by getting your shin to their belt on the outside of their legs  
**Constraints:** Must stay standing on your feet or in a proper base - no collapsing to knees or diving to hips  
**Win:** Successfully touch your shin to your partner's belt on the outside of their legs

## Instructions
1. Bottom player starts in Position A or B (randomly select)
2. Both players work simultaneously toward their respective goals
3. Bottom player prioritizes either getting hips down first OR hip connection first - both must be achieved to win
4. Top player must maintain standing/base position throughout - no dropping to knees or sitting
5. Top player must reach the outside hip position with shin-to-belt connection
6. If bottom player achieves both goals (hips down + hip connection) first, reset and switch roles
7. If top player gets shin to belt on outside first, reset and switch roles
8. On role switch, bottom player may start from Position A or B (different from last round if possible)
9. Continue for full time period

## Coaching Points
For bottom player:
- From foot-on-inside position: use foot pressure and ankle grips to control top player's hips and base
- From x-guard: drive your hip into top player's thigh and use the overhook to disrupt balance
- Work systematically - you need TWO things (mat and connection) so plan your sequence
- Use foot pressure to set up hip connection attempts
- Control hip height before attempting connection
- If top player is moving away, extend and collapse their base angles
- Use your hands to stay connected and prevent them from clearing your legs

For top player:
- Move with purpose toward the outside hip position
- Use angles and footwork to create space away from bottom player's grips
- Keep your posture strong and base wide - don't let them pull you forward
- Work methodically around their leg guard toward the outside
- Once you identify the path to the outside, commit to reaching shin-to-belt
- Defend against them falling you by maintaining your base and staying on your feet
- Look for opportunities to pass completely when they overcommit to one goal
