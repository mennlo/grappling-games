# Side Control Framing to Submission

## Overview
A pinning drill where the bottom player works to maintain frames at the hip and neck to prevent control and escape, while the top player attempts to break frames, establish dominant control, and secure submissions from any top position while staying out of guard.

## Details
**Time:** 3 minutes, 2 partners or 6 minutes total - switching back and forth  

### Top Player
**Position:** On top, in front of your partner's legs hugging their hips
**Objective:** Break the bottom player's frames, establish control, and work to submit from any dominant position (side control, mount, knee on belly, back control, etc.) while staying out of their guard  
**Constraints:**  
**Win:** Successfully submit the bottom player with any submission while maintaining a dominant position (side control, mount, knee on belly, back control, etc.)

### Bottom Player
**Position:** On your side, framing at the shoulder and wrist 
**Objective:** Maintain frames to prevent control, then work to escape and recover guard or reverse position  
**Constraints:**  
**Win:** Get your legs back in between you and your partner with at least one of the top player's legs controlled above the knee, or successfully get on top  

## Instructions
1. Bottom player starts on their back with frames at the hip and neck
2. Top player starts on top, in front of the bottom player's legs
3. Bottom player works to maintain their frames and prevent crossface and underhook control
4. Top player works to break frames and establish dominant control position
5. Top player can transition between side control, mount, knee on belly, and back control to hunt for submissions
6. Top player must stay out of guard - if bottom player controls a leg above the knee with their legs, bottom player wins
7. Bottom player must control at least one leg above the knee (ankle traps don't count) to win via guard recovery
8. If bottom player achieves guard with leg control or becomes top player, they win the round
9. If top player submits bottom player from any top position, they win the round
10. Reset and continue for the full time period
11. After time expires, switch roles
