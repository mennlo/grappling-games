# Progressive Mount Wrist Control to Submission

## Overview
A progressive drilling game starting from mount with wrist control where the top player works to maintain control and secure armbar or arm triangle submissions from any top position while staying out of guard. Partners work through three different starting positions and alternate roles as they achieve success.

## Details
**Time:** 3 minutes, 2 partners or 6 minutes total - switching back and forth

### Position Progression
Players start at Position 1 and progress to the next position after winning. When either player wins any position, switch roles.

### Attacking Player
**Objective:** Get to an armbar position or arm triangle position  
**Win Condition:** Armbar position secured OR arm triangle position secured (finish if agreed)

**Starting Positions:**
- Position 1: Mount with 1 wrist pinned to the ground
- Position 2: Mount with wrist grips on chest
- Position 3: Side control

**Allowed Submissions:**
- Armbar (from any position)
- Arm triangle (from any position)

### Defending Player
**Objective:** Get both your knees and hands in front of your opponent  
**Win Condition:** Both knees and both hands positioned in front of opponent (defensive frames with leg control) or successfully escape to top position

## Instructions
1. Both players start at Position 1
2. Attacking player works to maintain control and hunt for armbar or arm triangle positions
3. Attacking player can transition to side control, knee on belly, back control, or other top positions but must stay out of guard
4. Defending player works to free their wrists, establish defensive frames, and escape
5. Partners can agree before session whether to finish submissions or stop at position
6. When either player wins, switch roles
7. After switching roles, progress to the next position (1→2→3)
8. Continue through all three positions for the duration of the session
9. Run 2-6 minute sessions as needed
