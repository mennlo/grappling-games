# Progressive Mount Wrist Control to Isolation

## Overview
A progressive drilling game focusing on establishing wrist control from mount position and using it to isolate both of the bottom player's arms in preparation for submissions. Partners work through three different starting positions and alternate roles as they achieve success.

## Details
**Time:** 3 minutes, 2 partners or 6 minutes total - switching back and forth

### Position Progression
Players start at Position 1 and progress to the next position after winning. When either player wins any position, switch roles.

### Attacking Player
**Objective:** Get under both arms and isolate them bicep to ear  
**Win Condition:** Successfully isolate both arms bicep to ear for 3 seconds

**Starting Positions:**
- Position 1: Mount with 1 wrist pinned to the ground
- Position 2: Mount with wrist grips on chest
- Position 3: Side control

### Defending Player
**Objective:** Get both your knees and hands in front of your opponent  
**Win Condition:** Both knees and both hands positioned in front of opponent (defensive frames on hips with knees between you and opponent)

## Instructions
1. Both players start at Position 1
2. Attacking player works to establish control of both wrists and isolate both arms bicep to ear
3. Defending player works to free their wrists and get both knees and hands in front of opponent
4. When either player wins, switch roles
5. After switching roles, progress to the next position (1→2→3)
6. Continue through all three positions for the duration of the session
7. Run 2-6 minute sessions as needed
8. Reset to the current position after each win
