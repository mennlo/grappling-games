# Progressive Mount Submissions

## Overview
A progressive drilling class focusing on submission attacks from mount and side control, where partners work through three different starting positions and alternate roles as they achieve success.

## Details
**Time:** 2-6 minute sessions

### Position Progression
Players start at Position 1 and progress to the next position after winning. When either player wins any position, switch roles.

### Attacking Player
**Objective:** Get to a head and arm choke position or armbar position  
**Win Condition:** Head and arm choke position secured OR armbar position secured (finish if agreed)

**Starting Positions:**
- Position 1: Mount with both arms isolated (bicep to ear)
- Position 2: Mount with wrist grips on chest
- Position 3: Side control

### Defending Player
**Objective:** Get both your knees and hands in front of your opponent  
**Win Condition:** Both knees and both hands positioned in front of opponent or successfully escape to top position

## Instructions
1. Both players start at Position 1
2. Attacking player works to achieve head and arm choke or armbar position
3. Defending player works to get both knees and hands in front of opponent
4. Partners can agree before session whether to finish submissions or stop at position
5. When either player wins, switch roles
6. After switching roles, progress to the next position (1→2→3)
7. Continue through all three positions for the duration of the session
8. Run 2-6 minute sessions as needed
