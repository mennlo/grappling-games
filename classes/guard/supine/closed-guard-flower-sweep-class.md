# Closed Guard Flower Sweep Class
**Class Length:** 1 hour

This class develops closed guard attack mechanics with a focus on the flower sweep, starting with standing grip fighting and takedown skills, progressing through guard passing concepts, and culminating with flower sweep mechanics, arm trap attacks, and armbar control. The progression builds from standing control to specific closed guard attack sequences.

This class consists of progressive drills (games) that build upon each other. Each drill is available as an individual game file for reference.

1. [Locked Hands Control Battle](https://mennlo.github.io/grappling-games/md-viewer.html?file=games/standing/locked-hands-control-battle.md)
2. [Single Leg Takedown Variations](https://mennlo.github.io/grappling-games/md-viewer.html?file=games/standing/single-leg-takedown-variations.md)
3. [Progressive Guard Passing](https://mennlo.github.io/grappling-games/md-viewer.html?file=games/guard/passing/progressive-guard-passing.md)
4. Flower Sweep Drilling (inline drill — no separate game file)
5. [Closed Guard Flower Sweep and Submit](https://mennlo.github.io/grappling-games/md-viewer.html?file=games/guard/closed/closed-guard-flower-sweep-and-submit.md)
6. [Armbar Control and Escape](https://mennlo.github.io/grappling-games/md-viewer.html?file=games/pinning/armbar-control-and-escape.md)

**Total Drilling Time:** 36 minutes
- Standing Drills: 12 minutes
  - Locked Hands Control Battle: 6 minutes (2 x 3-minute rounds)
  - Single Leg Takedown Variations: 6 minutes (2 x 3-minute rounds)
- Guard Drills: 24 minutes
  - Progressive Guard Passing: 6 minutes (2 x 3-minute rounds)
  - Flower Sweep Drilling: 6 minutes (2 x 3-minute rounds)
  - Closed Guard Flower Sweep and Submit: 6 minutes (2 x 3-minute rounds)

## Standing Drills

### 1.1 - [Locked Hands Control Battle](https://mennlo.github.io/grappling-games/md-viewer.html?file=games/standing/locked-hands-control-battle.md)

**Overview:** A standing control drill where players compete to establish either upper body or lower body control by connecting their hands around their partner's body.

**Time:** 3 minutes, 2 partners or 6 minutes total - switching back and forth

#### Upper Body Controller
**Position:** Standing, facing your partner  
**Objective:** Connect your hands around your partner's upper body (torso, around the back)  
**Win:** Successfully lock your hands around your partner's upper body  

#### Lower Body Controller
**Position:** Standing, facing your partner  
**Objective:** Connect your hands around your partner's lower body (legs or hips)  
**Win:** Successfully lock your hands around your partner's lower body  

### 1.2 - [Single Leg Takedown Variations](https://mennlo.github.io/grappling-games/md-viewer.html?file=games/standing/single-leg-takedown-variations.md)

**Overview:** A progressive drilling game where partners work through three different single leg positions, alternating roles as they achieve success in each position.

**Time:** 3 minutes, 2 partners or 6 minutes total - switching back and forth

**Position Progression:**
Players start at Position 1 and progress to the next position after winning. When either player wins any position, switch roles.

- **Position 1:** Standard single leg grip - opponent's leg held with hands and arms between your legs, head on chest  
- **Position 2:** Ankle grip control on defender's leg  
- **Position 3:** Defender is in turtle position  

#### Attacking Player
**Win Condition:**
- Positions 1 & 2: Get defender's hips on the ground and cover the hips, OR get defender's hands on the mat and secure a rear bodylock
- Position 3: Get defender to a hip and cover the hips

#### Defending Player
**Win Condition:**
- Positions 1 & 2: Get both legs free and face your opponent
- Position 3: Turn and face your opponent

## Guard Drills

### 2.1 - [Progressive Guard Passing](https://mennlo.github.io/grappling-games/md-viewer.html?file=games/guard/passing/progressive-guard-passing.md)

**Overview:** A progressive guard passing drill where the top player works through three different guard starting positions, staying standing to touch their shin to the bottom player's belt on the outside, while the bottom player tries to get the top player down to their hips and stand up.

**Time:** 3 minutes, 2 partners or 6 minutes total - switching back and forth

**Position Progression:**
Players start at Position 1 and progress to the next position after winning. When either player wins any position, switch roles.

#### Top Player
**Objective:** Touch your shin to your partner's belt on the outside of their legs  
**Constraints:** Must stay standing throughout the attempt  
**Win:** Successfully touch shin to belt

#### Bottom Player
**Objective:** Get the top player down to their hips and stand up  
**Win:** Top player on hips and you standing

**Starting Positions:**
- Position 1: On your back with feet on the inside of top player's knees/thighs and hands on both ankles
- Position 2: On your back with legs wrapped around one leg, feet crossed behind top player's butt, and hand grip on the ankle
- Position 3: Seated with shin-on-shin guard established

### 2.2 - Flower Sweep Drilling

**Overview:** A cooperative drilling sequence breaking down the three key mechanics of the flower sweep. Partners work through each step in sequence, building from the initial angle creation through arm trapping and finishing with the pendulum kick. Drill cooperatively before adding resistance.

**Time:** 3 minutes, 2 partners or 6 minutes total - switching back and forth

#### Drilling Steps

**Step 1 — Ankle/Leg Grab + Hip Escape**  
Underhook or grab your partner's knee and bridge/shrimp your hips away from that knee to create the sweep angle. Focus on getting your hips out far enough to establish a clear angle toward the sweep direction.

**Step 2 — Trap the Arm**  
Secure the same-side arm you are sweeping toward by pulling it to your chest and pinning it so your partner cannot post out to stop the sweep. Keep the arm hugged tightly — any space allows them to post and block the roll.

**Step 3 — Pendulum Kick**  
Use your free leg as a pendulum, swinging it to generate momentum and kick your partner's torso over, rolling them in the direction of the trapped arm. Coordinate the pendulum kick with your hip angle and arm trap for maximum effect.

#### Coaching Points
- Complete each step in order before adding speed or resistance
- The hip escape in Step 1 is the foundation — without the angle, the sweep stalls
- Keep the trapped arm glued to your chest throughout the kick
- The pendulum leg should swing low to high, driving through your partner's body
- Once comfortable with the sequence, add light resistance and flow through the full sweep

### 2.3 - [Closed Guard Flower Sweep and Submit](https://mennlo.github.io/grappling-games/md-viewer.html?file=games/guard/closed/closed-guard-flower-sweep-and-submit.md)

**Overview:** A closed guard drill where the bottom player starts with one of the top player's arms trapped to their chest, working to complete a sweep, submission, or back take, while the top player works to escape the arm trap and pass the guard to side control.

**Time:** 3 minutes, 2 partners or 6 minutes total - switching back and forth

#### Bottom Player
**Position:** On your back in closed guard with one of the top player's arms trapped to your chest  
**Objective:** Sweep, submit, or take the back from the arm trap position  
**Win:** Successfully complete a sweep, land a submission, or establish back control with both hooks in

#### Top Player
**Position:** On your knees inside closed guard with one arm trapped to your partner's chest  
**Objective:** Escape the arm trap and pass the guard to side control  
**Win:** Successfully pass the guard and establish side control

### 2.4 - [Armbar Control and Escape](https://mennlo.github.io/grappling-games/md-viewer.html?file=games/pinning/armbar-control-and-escape.md)

**Overview:** A submission control drill where the top player starts in an armbar position and works to control, isolate, and straighten the arm, while the bottom player attempts to escape without being submitted.

**Time:** 3 minutes, 2 partners or 6 minutes total - switching back and forth

#### Top Player
**Position:** On your back or side with one leg over partner's torso, one leg over their head, and hands connected to partner's arm at the elbow  
**Objective:** Maintain tight connection with partner's shoulders while splitting their hands apart to isolate and straighten the arm  
**Win:** Successfully split the hands apart, straighten the arm, and maintain control for 3 seconds before applying breaking pressure

#### Bottom Player
**Position:** On your back in an armbar position with hands connected in your preferred defensive grip  
**Objective:** Escape from the armbar position using any available technique without being submitted  
**Win:** Successfully escape the armbar position without getting submitted

## Conclusion

Finish class with Q&A and some live rolling starting from closed guard position. Focus on applying the flower sweep mechanics learned during class, emphasizing the importance of creating the angle, trapping the arm, and timing the pendulum kick with the opponent's reactions. Bottom players should practice the full sweep-to-submission chain and look for back takes when the sweep is defended. Top players should practice freeing the trapped arm early and driving to pass immediately once the guard opens.
